Website Evaluations

Apple

Apple's website is sleek, modern, and easy to navigate, showcasing their products effectively.

  • Contrast: Strong contrast, easy to read.
  • Repetition: Consistent fonts and colors.
  • Alignment: Well-structured layout.
  • Proximity: Related elements grouped together.

Apple effectively uses all CRAP principles.

Penny Juice

Penny Juice is a wholesale distributor of fruit juice concentrates for childcare centers.

  • Contrast: Bright backgrounds with vibrant text cause readability issues.
  • Repetition: Inconsistent design elements across pages.
  • Alignment: Poor layout with haphazard placement of text and images.
  • Proximity: Related information is not effectively grouped together.

Penny Juice's website fails to adhere to the CRAP principles, leading to a confusing user experience.
